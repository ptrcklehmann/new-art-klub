NEW ART KLUB

an emerging collective, a platform for artistic exchange

new art klub is a collective project, supporting an eclectic mix of musicians, artists and writers. producing events at various locations in berlin, new art club showcases interdisciplinary art to inspires others and foster contemplation. new art klub also operates as an informal platform where everyone is invited to share and discuss their ideas on art and beyond. we are a non-governmental organization, instead we strive to unite and stimulate innovative artistry by creative means!

Fantasie an die Macht

New art klub proudly announces its second exhibition FANTASIE AN DIE MACHT.

FANTASIE AN DIE MACHT is an interdisciplinary art project, taking place at Modular+, the open art space on the 8th. floor of the artist residence Green House Berlin, inviting artists to challenge, question and play with the notions of Creativity and Power. In a world which is becoming increasingly unstable, where the idea of truth has been turned on its head, art as a form of expression has become more important than ever.

New art klub asks artists and spectators to join the movement and engage in the discussion on the power of the fantasy.

Knowing so Wrong, Feeling so Right

offering opportunities for attendants to interact with the works and the immersive atmosphere that has been created, 'knowing so wrong, feeling so right' is less about the artist's intention but more about allowing visitors to contemplate on their own deeper realities.

'knowing so wrong, feeling so right' is also a tribute to the tradition of "the art and the party" thus it will be an evening of discussion and collaboration as well as celebration and friendship.
